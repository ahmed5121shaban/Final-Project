Zaid is a premier auction site where you can buy and sell a variety of items with ease and confidence.

Welcome to Zaid, your premier online auction marketplace where history and innovation meet. We connect buyers and sellers from all corners of Europe, offering a vast selection of furniture, art, design pieces, collectibles, and more.

Unlike conventional online marketplaces, our auctions are meticulously curated. Each item is inspected, described, and photographed by experts to ensure you can bid with confidence. Our commitment to quality means you can trust that every item meets our high standards.

Choosing to buy at auction is not just a smart choice for your budget, but also for the environment. Discover unique items that add character to your home while supporting sustainable practices. And if you decide to sell, you’re likely to recoup your investment or even make a profit.

We bring the charm of local auction houses to the global stage. Whether you're bidding on an antique from southern Germany or a vintage piece from northern Sweden, you can do so with ease. Our transparent shipping costs and secure payment options on Zaid.com ensure a seamless experience. For any queries, our multilingual support team is always ready to assist.
